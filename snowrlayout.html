What is the SnowRunner app?

SnowRunner Mobile is the best mobile version of the popular off-road simulator that started out as MudRunner for PCs and consoles. Players have to keep their car’s weight, damage, and petrol levels in check while driving over mud, snow, rivers, and steep terrain. People who love to drive off-road on their phones are mostly interested in this game because of its carefully made trucks and realistic physics engine.
